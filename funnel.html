Where businesses lose leads

Your next client should not disappear inside chats, inboxes, or forgotten follow-ups.

Leak 01

Visitors get sent to a general website.

They see many pages, many buttons, and no urgent reason to take action now.

Leak 02

Inquiries are scattered everywhere.

Messages arrive from social media, email, calls, and forms without one clean lead record.

Leak 03

Follow-up depends on memory.

When the team is busy, hot leads are replied to late or forgotten completely.

The Mary Digi Approach

Build a page that sells the next step, not a page that explains everything.

A landing page funnel does not need to show every company detail. It needs to make the visitor understand the problem, trust the solution, and take the next action.
